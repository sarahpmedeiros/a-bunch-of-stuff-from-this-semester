“Marriott Custom House - Spectacular !”
Reviewed September 20, 2016

Spectacular, truly the only word needed to describe the Marriott Custom House located at 3 McKinley Square, Boston. We recently spent one week there, and everything was top notch. The property itself is so full of a very interesting history; be sure to take the "Custom House Tour" with Matt. The rooms are beautiful. The property just completed a full makeover in 2016. All / most rooms are one bedroom, one bath, with a pull out couch. The kitchens have a small refrig., microwave, coffee maker, sink, and all plates, glasses, utensils, blender customary to any Marriott timeshare. ( no stove, oven or dishwasher ). The amenities here are great: Exercise room, Game room, Mini theater room, Kids play area, and a true highlight is the observation deck on the 26th floor. You get a 360 degree view of Boston. Be sure to visit during the day and at night to see all the lights of the Boston harbor and the city. This property is one block from Faneuil Hall and Quincy Market, 2 blocks from the waterfront and a short walk to the North End Italian section. We always used to stay at the Marriott in the Copley Square area ( very nice ), but we will always stay at this location for future visits to Boston. Be sure to take advantage of all the free activities at the location. Whether it be the nightly "cocktail tastings", the walking city tours ( Diane is a great tour guide !), the nightly music events or even the nightly movies, you will never be at a loss for something to do right at the property ! Most importantly, everyone working at the Custom House treats you like a very valued guest. You will be made to feel very special ! The front desk staff, the concierge's, the activities personnel, the movie room operator, the breakfast hostess, the bartender, the valet, housekeeping etc., are all just wonderful. Marriott has set a new bar for excellence when it comes to a city located property. Do yourself a favor, whether your first trip or a returning trip to Boston, stay at the Custom House.

Room Tip: Request a high floor, regardless of what you face, the view will be excellent

“First time at Pulse and was just about ideal”
Reviewed September 8, 2016

Asked for a high floor with harbour view and that is exactly what we got. Staff were friendly, helpful, and conscientious. Was pleasantly surprised when the front desk called up to our room shortly after check-in to make sure we liked the room.

We had a king room, which was well appointed with modern decor. It is a two room suite, with a good size bedroom and living-room. Flat screen HDTVs in both rooms. There is a nice kitchen/dining table and a small kitchenette with microwave, toaster, coffee-maker and nice dishes and utensils. Its not a full kitchen if you are hoping to cook meals, but there are tons of nearby restaurants and take out establishments, so just enjoy what the city has to offer. It is a beautiful location, close to most points of interest.

Minor drawbacks inlcude;
- expensive parking ($40 per day + tax)
- no pool
- Just 2 elevators though they did move quickly.
- breakfast is continental and at a cost.

Pluses include;
- location, location, location
- modern and beautifully furnished rooms
- excellent staff
- hotel activities/socials
- observation deck
- historic building
- great rates for Marriott Reward Members.

Room Tip: Pick a high floor to minimize street noise.
